monologue

Hercules Achilles

I was going to call these my “mythological songs”, but that would be illogical because these are real songs. Ideally, I’d start with a monologue about enlightenment or something cool like that… but I’m not “enlightened”, and I’m not even sure I believe in the kind of blinding light epiphany where you jump up and yell “Eureka!”. The one time I saw the light it was quickly turned off. I’m the guy who accidentally made it up the mountain, met the master, heard the koan, and headed out the cave door and back down the hill. I’m more of a believer in a continuum of consciousness. The problem is you can’t go from Here to There. It’s a Be Here Now thing. How hard can that be? It’s not a long trip, unless you do the Zeno’s arrow thing: Get half way there today. Cut the distance in half tomorrow… .That’s me. Edging ever closer but probably just too afraid to Be Here. I must be Zenophobic. I know I’m Xylophobic, because xylophones scare the hell out of me. Think about it. . . Do caves even have doors?

God invented Koans about the same time he—and I say “he” for those who think there’s some big guy sitting on a golden throne while watching naked angels dance and stroking his long … beard… I think that’s as likely as a chubby, no-nothing super tweeter with orange hair becoming president… Well anyway, after creating mind-exploding brain puzzles God invented apple trees and snakes. Then she invented Beer. And Sex. And Pepper. I really like pepper.

Angel music

Twilight Zone If not, I can give up on my Here and Now Koan and invent Apple Flavored Pepper, become rich, and then buy my own stairway to heaven or just hurdle myself through the eye of a needle before I get too fat. Money is power. With enough of it I could buy my own throne, naked angels, and an orange wig. Stairway to Heaven
